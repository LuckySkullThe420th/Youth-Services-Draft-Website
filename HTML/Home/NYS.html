Restorative Justice
The Juvenile Review Board is a Juvenile Court Diversion System that offers youth who have broken the law (or who are at risk) a positive and meaningful alternative to the Criminal Justice System

Care Coordination Services
Primarily Care Coordination focuses on behavioral/mental health needs and referred to appropiate services. In addition, referrals may also be offered for mentorship, youth development, and Parks and Recreational programs.

Family Navigation
Family Navigation is a program offered to all Norwalk Residents; A trusted relationship to connect individuals and families to community resources.

Mayor's Summer Youth Program
The Mayor's Summer Youth Program goes through a arduous training week before getting jobs. They push teens out of their comfort zone through mock interviews, creative team discussion, and introductions. They are then sent out to thrive in the workplace as interns, where they gain work experience as well as earn money.
